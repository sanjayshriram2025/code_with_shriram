How Bin-e works

Bin-e: Where AI meets ecology

Our flagship product uses state-of-the-art technology and artificial intelligence to make waste management more efficient. The device automatically segregates waste.
AI-based Waste recognition
Bin-e uses an artificial intelligence-based recognition system to identify waste, becoming a global market leader with a 95% segregation accuracy.
Automatic waste sorting
The recognized waste will be then transported to the right fraction. Everything happens 100% automatically.
Compression of plastic & paper
Plastic and paper get compressed to decrease the volume and lower the frequency of bin emptying.
Fill level control and alerts
Fill level sensors check if the container needs emptying. You will receive an e-mail notification when the containers are full.
Real-time data management
All the data Bin-e collects is transferred onto a cloud and processed in real-time.
App for stats & reports
Our app provides you detailed statistics about the waste you collected, data for optimizing waste collection and the technical status your devices.
