About CodeWithShriram

At CodeWithShriram, we believe learning should be practical and impactful. Founded by Sanjay Shriram of Panimalar Engineering College, our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world skills.

We are a student-driven initiative offering free internships, live projects, and a supportive community. Our programs focus on hands-on learning, ensuring every participant applies theory in practical scenarios.
